Investigating Predator-Prey Model of Lynx and Hare

Umay Gokturk, David Ou, Martyna Wojciechowska 2025 Differential Equations
Abstract: This project constructs a Lotka-Volterra predator-prey inverse problem to analyze the dynamical relationship between Lynx and Hare populations using historical data from Hudson Bay, Canada (1845-1935). We set up an inverse problem to estimate the model's best-fitting parameters using a weighted sum of squared errors loss function. To enhance realism, we extend the model with damping terms accounting for intra-species competition and environmental constraints. The study compares fitted models with real-world data and evaluates predictive power and limitations.
